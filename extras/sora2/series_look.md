# Series Look Locks (Sora2)

## Frame & Motion
- Aspect: 16:9 (1920×1080 or 3840×2160). FPS: 30. Duration per clip: 10–12s.
- Camera movement: subtle dolly or slider (0.2–0.4 m over full shot), ease-in/out; no whip pans.
- Stabilization: locked horizon; minimal handheld wobble.
- Loopability: first/last 10 frames visually similar where possible.

## Optics & Focus
- Virtual lens: 35mm (FF equiv) for mediums; 50mm for closeups. Aperture f/2.8–f/4 for shallow but readable DOF.
- Focus pulls: one gentle pull max per shot, 1–1.5s duration.

## Lighting
- Neutral-cool key (4800–5200K), softbox feel. Gentle rim light. Practical accents allowed (warm 3000K).
- No flicker, no hard speculars on text overlays.

## Color & Grade
- Palette anchors: Slate Navy #0F172A, Graphite #2B2E34, Off-White #F5F7FA.
- Accents (sparingly ≤10% frame): Cool Teal #2DA8A3, Soft Gold #E2C044, Safety Red #D14D4D for callouts only.
- Grade: low-contrast cinematic, slight filmic grain (8–10%), vignette −10.

## Graphic Continuity
- Overlay safe-margins: 8% from edges. Lower-third blocks: 85% width max.
- Typography: high-legibility geometric sans (system/open-source), bold for headers, medium for body.
- Iconography: abstract shapes only. **No brand marks, no currency logos, no government seals.**

## Motifs
- Soft parallax grids, flowing particle “data” lines, hex/circuit meshes with faint bloom.
- Transition language: cross-dissolve or match-cut motion only.

## Safety & Ratings
- PG-13 visual language. No faces identifiable; no sensitive signage; no real-world brands or logos.

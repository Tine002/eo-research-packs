Executive Order 14067 (Mar. 9, 2022) sets a government-wide approach to digital assets. Highlights:

• **Secs. 1–2:** policy interests and objectives across protection, stability, illicit finance, rights, inclusion, and climate/energy  
• **Sec. 3:** APNSA/APEP coordinate an interagency process across departments and independent regulators  
• **Sec. 4:** research and assessment on a potential U.S. CBDC (study, not issuance)  
• **Secs. 5–8:** assessments on consumer protection, market integrity, financial stability, illicit finance, and international cooperation

Summary

Executive Order 14067 (Mar. 9, 2022) states a whole-of-government approach to digital assets. It outlines policy interests and objectives (Secs. 1ΓÇô2), assigns interagency coordination to the APNSA and APEP (Sec. 3), instructs research on a potential U.S. CBDC (Sec. 4), and directs assessments related to consumer protection, market integrity, financial stability, illicit finance, and international cooperation (Secs. 5ΓÇô8). It does not authorize new spending or create new agencies.

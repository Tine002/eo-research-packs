# Policy status snapshot: then vs now

**Then (2022): EO 14067** — launched a coordinated study program across agencies on digital assets and CBDC tradeoffs. <PIN_FR14067_OVERVIEW>

**Now (2025): EO 14178** — revoked 14067 in Sec. 3 and reset language, including explicit text that distances from or prohibits a retail CBDC approach. <PIN_14178_SEC3_TEXT>

# EO 14067 — Ensuring Responsible Development of Digital Assets

**Primary sources:**  
- Federal Register: https://www.federalregister.gov/documents/2022/03/14/2022-05471/ensuring-responsible-development-of-digital-assets  
- GovInfo PDF: https://www.govinfo.gov/content/pkg/FR-2022-03-14/pdf/2022-05471.pdf

## Summary
Executive Order 14067 (Mar. 9, 2022) states a whole-of-government approach to digital assets. It outlines policy interests and objectives (Secs. 1–2), assigns interagency coordination to the APNSA and APEP (Sec. 3), instructs research on a potential U.S. CBDC (Sec. 4), and directs assessments related to consumer protection, market integrity, financial stability, illicit finance, and international cooperation (Secs. 5–8). It does not authorize new spending or create new agencies.
